Hypernova Majorfighters

Hypernova Majorfighters is a cooperative third-person shooter game where you can choose one of 8 characters. Every single character has its own unique abilities, playing style, purpose, strengths and weaknesses. The goal is to find and destroy the boss, by killing enemies, spending earned money and experience for buying more powerful weapons and improving the ability of the character.
